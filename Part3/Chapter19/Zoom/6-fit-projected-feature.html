Zoom to projected feature

This example fits a selected feature, as projected, in the viewport. The projection is unchanged, so the feature is scaled up as it appears in the current projection (it may be distorted or incomplete if near the edges of the map). Click on any country to zoom to it. Double-click anywhere to unzoom.

Note that a feature may include objects that are distant from the clicked shape (e.g. France also includes French Guyana, so clicking on France will select a large area of the Atlantic ocean).
