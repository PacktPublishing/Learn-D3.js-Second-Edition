Dragging, rotating, and zooming a map in D3

This implementation attempts an interactive rotation using mouse coordinates. You can zoom in and out and rotate the map by dragging the mouse. All effects are implemented using d3.zoom and two of the three euler angles (yaw, pitch, roll). Since only two angles are used, it is limited in the way it applies the rotation, and can produce unexpected results when dragging in certain directions. For example, if you rotate the map by 90 degrees, the rotation will be applied in the wrong direction.
