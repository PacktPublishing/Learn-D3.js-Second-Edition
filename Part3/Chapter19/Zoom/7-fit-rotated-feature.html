Zoom to rotated feature

This example fits a selected feature in the viewport, but first rotates the globe so that the feature's centroid occupies the center of the projection. This allows for minimum distortion in any projection (try it with Orthographic and Gnomonic). Click on any country and the globe will rotate so that if appears with the smallest distortion. Double-click to unzoom.

Note that a feature may include objects that are distant from the clicked shape (e.g. France also includes French Guyana, so clicking on France will select a large area of the Atlantic ocean). These features will also be displayed with more distortion, since they won't be in the center.
