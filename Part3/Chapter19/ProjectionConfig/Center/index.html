Changing the geographic center

Examples using the projection's center() method.

  • Default center. Geo center (equator x prime meridian) at 240,480.
  • Center-Tokyo. Center changed to Tokyo coordinates.
  • Center-India. Center changed to India centroid.
  • Center-Tokyo with scale. Tokyo at center and zoomed in.
  • Center-India with scale. india at center and zoomed in.
  • Center-Tokyo with scale and translate. Geo center + viewport transforms.
  • Center-India with scale, angle and translate. Geo center + viewport transforms.
