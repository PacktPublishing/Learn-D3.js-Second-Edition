Clipping an Orthographic projection

Using clipAngle(degrees) to apply clipping to an Orthographic projection. By default, this projection is clipped at 90 degrees, so that only the part of the globe that would be visible to the viewer in perspective is shown. By expanding it, the opposite parts of the globe are shown. By reducing it, you can clip a section of the globe (this example is centered on Iceland.) The lighter map is the default projection (90 degree clipping), shown as a reference.
