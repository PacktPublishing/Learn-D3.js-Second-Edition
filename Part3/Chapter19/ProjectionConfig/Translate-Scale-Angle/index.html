Viewport translation, scaling and rotation

Use these methods to transform a projected map (using pixel coordinates).

  • Default translation. Geo center (equator x prime meridian) at 240,480.
  • Translate to (0,0) pixels. Move geo center to top-left viewport position.
  • Translate to middle of first quadrant. Moves geo center down and right.
  • Default scale for the Mercator projection.
  • Zoom out scale 75. Fits complete Mercator projection
  • Zoom in scale 160. Slightly more than Mercator default.
  • Zoom in 480. Applying scale(480).
  • Zoom in 1000. Applying scale(1000).
  • Rotate Mercator by 45 deg.
  • Rotate Mercator by 90 deg.
  • Rotate Orthographic projection by 45 deg.
