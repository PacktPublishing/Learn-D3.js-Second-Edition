Fitting methods

Use these methods to fit a projection in a viewport, based on extent (top-left and bottom-right pixel coordinates), size (width and height), or a single dimnsion (height or width).

  • fitExtent applied to the globe
  • fitExtent applied to a "tall" GeoJSON feature
  • fitExtent applied to a "wide" GeoJSON feature
  • fitSize applied to the globe
  • fitSize applied to a "tall" GeoJSON feature
  • fitSize applied to a "wide" GeoJSON feature
  • fitWidth applied to the globe
  • fitWidth applied to a "tall" GeoJSON feature
  • fitWidth applied to a "wide" GeoJSON feature
  • fitHeight applied to the globe
  • fitHeight applied to a "tall" GeoJSON feature
  • fitHeight applied to a "wide" GeoJSON feature
