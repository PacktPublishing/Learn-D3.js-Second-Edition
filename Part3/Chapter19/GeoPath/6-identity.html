The identity projection

Creating a geoPath and not assigning a projection will assign the identity projection (d3.geoIdentity() as the current projection. This projection will treat longitudes as x coordinates, and latitudes as y coordinates. The final map will have dimensions of 360 by 180 pixels.

Since the viewport's origin is located at top-left, it will contain the center of the map, which is also projected with an inverted longitude (pointing downwards). The reflectY() method can be used to fix this, and translating the map to the center of the viewport will display it completely. Click to see this effect. Click again to revert.
