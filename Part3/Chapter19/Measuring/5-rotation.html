Example using d3.geoRotation()

Use d3.geoRotation() functions to move objects on the globe.

Choose an initial target point (Longitude, Latitude) and set it. Then choose a rotation (yaw, pitch, roll) and press a button to apply the rotation or its inverse to change the current target point. Open the console for more results.

Initial target: [Lon , Lat ]

Rotation = [Yaw , Pitch , Roll ]
