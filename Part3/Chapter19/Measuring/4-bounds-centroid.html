Bounds and centroids of features

This visualization shows bounds and centroids of features using planar coordinates (using geoPath.bounds() and geoPath.centroid()) and in geographical coordinates (using d3.geoBounds() and d3.geoCentroid()). The bounds are rendered as rectangles that completely contain the feature and the centroid is its geometric center. Spherical coordinates do not change with the projection. Planar coordinates can change dramatically depending on the projection and rotation.

Rotation = [Yaw , Pitch , Roll ]
