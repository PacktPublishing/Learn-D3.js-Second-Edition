Satellite orbit with d3-geoRotation()

This example demonstrates an object moving over the globe. The object leaves a place on the Earth (Baikonur, Kazakhstan) and then orbits at 51.6 degrees inclination, drifting 20 degrees of latitude eastward every time. The launch from the initial site, the orbit, and the drift are all implemented with d3.geoRotation().
