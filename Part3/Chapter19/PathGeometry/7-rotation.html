Example using d3.rotation()

Choose a viewport position (Longitude, Latitude) and a rotation (yaw, pitch, roll) and press the button. In 500ms three circles will be drawn at the position. After 500ms a blue circle will move to the rotated position, and after another 500ms the red circle will move to the inverse of the original position (opposite rotation).
